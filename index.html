Purdue University

Research Assistant in Computational Solid Mechanics Lab

✔ Failure in Fiber Reinforced Polymer Composites

Fiber reinforced composite is gaining increasing popularity in light weighting application. The goal of this project is to develop a multi-scale numerical method, Phase Field Damage Model (PFDM), to predict the failure initiation and propagation in polymer matrix composite.
✔ Defects in Nano and Ultrafine Grained Metals
Ultrafine and nano-grained metals show plastic strain recovery upon unloading and reverse plastic strain during cyclic loading. The goal of this project is to understand and predict this interesting phenomenon, which could be of critical importance for the long-term reliability and survivability of microelectro-mechanical systems (MEMS).
